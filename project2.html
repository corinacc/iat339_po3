OLFACTORY

A Luxurious Niche Magazine About Perfume.

About the Project

This project was created from PUB 332, a course primarily focused

on Graphic Design in transition: Print and Digital Periodicals.

This project is to envision and design a single publication. I decided

to make a perfume magzine because I am passionate about the scent.

Skills Gained

  • Editorial Mandate & Brand Design
  • Editorial Lineup &Flatplan Design
  • Adobe InDesign software

Firstly, I create a mood board and a grid system for my Cover Story Editorial Feature. I collected magazine layouts, typrograph, brand logo and images that are related to my topic and classified them into groups of Photography, Typography, Colour Palette, Layout + Space, in order to plan a possible brand logo and grid system for my layout. I did the search about magazine layouts and images that are related to my topic and collected them.

I brainstorm the potential meaningful name and the font for the brand. Then, I started to try different types of font to see which font will fit the feeling of my magazine. Also, I read many chic magazines and learn how they plan present their content on the cover page of magazine.

I created a complete flat plan for an 84-page magazine. My flat plan completed flatplan will thus have 41 pages of editorial and 43 pages of advertisements. The blue box represents the ads and the white box represents the editorial page of the magazine.

Process

I started to create the table of content based on my editorial Line up and the flat plan. I use a clear and flexible grid structure and allow for negative space and group articles under departments with clearly labelled headings. Also, I tried flexible layouts to ensure that work well when the articles change slightly with each issue and highlighted the select article with imagery.

Then I start to create the Editorial Feature page.

Reflection

I met a challenge with my initial brand name. I changed the name of my brand after I found that my first choice “SCENT” already existed as an account on instagram. So I have to come out with another new brand name “OLFACTORY”; It is important to do the sufficient research on all platforms and websites before starting a project.
